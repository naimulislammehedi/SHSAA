The Legacy

Empowering our graduates to lead and inspire.

The Silonia High School Alumni Association was established to maintain a lifelong connection between the school and its former students. We believe that our collective strength can pave the way for a brighter future.

Our Mission

To foster a vibrant community where alumni can support each other professionally and contribute to school development.

Our Vision

To create a globally recognized network of successful professionals rooted in the values of Silonia High School.
